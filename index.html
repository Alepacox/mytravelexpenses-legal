Privacy Policy — MyTravelExpenses

Last updated: 26 May 2026

MyTravelExpenses (“the app”) is a personal travel-expense tracker. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. Please read it carefully.

Summary

  • Your missions, expenses, and receipt files are stored only on your device.
  • The app has no user accounts and no developer-operated servers. We do not collect, transmit, or store your expense data on any server we control.
  • The app shows ads via Google AdMob, which may collect data as described below.
  • The app makes a small number of requests to third-party services (Wikipedia, OpenStreetMap) to fetch destination images and city suggestions.

Data stored on your device

The following stays local to your device and is never sent to us:

  • Expenses and missions — amounts, dates, merchants, categories, notes, budgets, destinations.
  • Receipt files — photos, scans, or PDFs you attach, saved in the app's private storage.
  • Settings — your name, email, default currency, language, theme, and funding sources, used only to fill in the expense reports you generate.

You can delete this data at any time by deleting missions/expenses inside the app or by uninstalling the app.

Camera and photos

The app requests camera access so you can scan or photograph receipts, and photo/file access so you can attach existing images or PDFs. Receipt images are processed on your device (text recognition runs locally via Google ML Kit). Receipt content is not uploaded to us or to any third party.

Export and import

You can export one or more missions to a .mte file and import them back. This file is created and shared at your request through your device's share sheet or file picker. Where that file goes afterwards (email, cloud drive, messaging app) is controlled entirely by you.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays banner ads served by Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google may collect and process data including your device's advertising identifier, IP address, and ad-interaction data, in accordance with Google's policies:

  • How Google uses data: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Where required by law (e.g. in the EEA, UK, and certain US states), the app shows a consent prompt (Google User Messaging Platform) before personalized ads are served. You can change your choice later via the in-app consent option where available.

Third-party network requests

To enrich the experience the app contacts:

  • Wikipedia REST API — to fetch a representative photo for a mission's destination. Only the city name is sent.
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — to suggest real places as you type a destination. Only your typed text is sent.

No personal expense data is included in these requests.

Children

The app is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect data from children under the age required by your local law.

Data security

Data stored locally is protected by your device's own security (lock screen, sandboxing). Because we operate no servers, there is no developer-side database that could be breached.

Your rights

Since your data lives only on your device, you control it directly: edit or delete it in-app, or uninstall to remove everything. For ad-related data, refer to the Google links above to exercise choices.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the most recent change.

Contact
